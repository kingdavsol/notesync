Getting Started
How do I create my first note?

Tap the green + button at the bottom-right of the Notes screen. A new note editor will open where you can type a title and start writing immediately. Your note saves automatically as you type — no save button needed.

How do I sync my notes across devices?

NoteSync syncs automatically whenever you're connected to the internet. You can also pull down on the notes list to trigger a manual sync, or tap Sync Now in Settings.

Each note shows a small colored dot indicating its sync status: green = fully synced, orange = waiting to sync.

Can I use NoteSync offline?

Yes. All your notes are stored locally on your device using an offline-first database. You can read and edit notes with no internet connection. Changes sync automatically when you reconnect.

How do I sign in or create an account?

Open NoteSync and tap Create one on the login screen to register with your email and a password (minimum 8 characters). If you already have an account, enter your email and password and tap Sign In.

Writing & Formatting
How do I format text (bold, italic, underline)?

When you tap into the note body, a formatting toolbar appears above the keyboard with these buttons:

  • B — Bold (wraps text in **markers**)
  • I — Italic (wraps text in *markers*)
  • U — Underline
  • S — Strikethrough
  • H — Highlight
  • H1 / H2 — Headings
  • List and numbered list buttons

Select text first to wrap it, or tap a button to insert a formatted placeholder.

How does the checklist mode work?

Tap the checkbox icon (✓□) in the bottom toolbar to toggle checklist mode on. While active, every time you press Enter, the next line automatically starts with a ☐ checkbox. Tap the icon again to turn it off. The checklist button turns green when active.

How do I attach a photo to a note?

In the note editor, tap the image icon to pick from your gallery, or the camera icon to take a new photo. The image appears below your text. Long-press a thumbnail to remove it.

How do I use voice transcription?

Tap the microphone icon in the toolbar. Hold the record button to speak — NoteSync uses AI (Groq Whisper) to transcribe your speech into text and insert it into your note. Works best in quiet environments with clear speech.

Organization
How do I search for a note?

Use the search bar at the top of the Notes screen. It searches note titles and content in real time as you type. Tap ✕ to clear the search.

How do I pin a note to the top?

Long-press any note in the list and tap Pin to top. Pinned notes appear in a separate "PINNED" section above your other notes, with a green left border. Long-press again and tap Unpin to move it back.

How do I use notebooks (folders)?

Tap the Notebooks tab at the bottom. Tap + to create a new notebook, then tap a notebook to view or rename it. You can assign a note to a notebook while editing (tag selector).

How do I use tags?

While editing a note, tap the tag icon in the toolbar. Type a new tag name and tap +, or select an existing tag. Tags appear as green chips below the note title and can be used to filter your search.

How do I delete a note?

There are two ways: (1) Open the note and tap the trash icon in the top-right corner. (2) Long-press the note in the list and tap Delete. Deleted notes sync the deletion to the server.

How do I share a note?

Open the note and tap the share icon (arrow pointing up) in the top-right. This opens your phone's native share sheet to send the note title and text via any app (Messages, Email, WhatsApp, etc.).

Account & Billing
What are the plan limits?
  • Free: 50 notes, 2 devices, 10 voice transcriptions/month
  • Starter ($2.99/mo): 500 notes, 5 devices, 50 transcriptions/month
  • Power ($7.99/mo): Unlimited notes & devices, unlimited transcription
How do I change my password?

Password changes are not yet available in-app. Please contact support at support@notesync.app and we'll assist you.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions can be cancelled through the Google Play Store (Android) billing settings. Go to Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

Is my data backed up if I lose my phone?

Yes. Every note is synced to our secure servers whenever you're online. After installing NoteSync on a new device and logging in, your notes will sync down automatically within seconds.
