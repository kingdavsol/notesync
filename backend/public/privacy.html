Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Last updated: February 26, 2026  ·  Effective: February 26, 2026

1. Who We Are

NoteSync ("we", "us", "our") is a note-taking application operated by Traffic2uMarketing. Our backend is hosted at notesync.9gg.app. For privacy inquiries, contact us at privacy@notesync.app.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account data: Email address and hashed password (we never store plaintext passwords).
  • Note content: Titles, text bodies, tags, folder names, and metadata (created/updated timestamps, pin status).
  • Images: Photos attached to notes are stored as base64-encoded data associated with your account.
  • Voice recordings: Audio recorded for transcription is transmitted to our transcription service and immediately discarded after processing — we do not retain audio files.
  • Device identifiers: A randomly generated device ID to enable multi-device sync conflict resolution.
  • Usage logs: Authentication events (login, logout, registration) are logged for security auditing. We do not log note content access.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the NoteSync service.
  • To sync your notes across your registered devices.
  • To authenticate you and protect your account from unauthorized access.
  • To transcribe voice recordings into text using our AI provider.
  • To send important service emails (verification, security alerts) — no marketing emails without your consent.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or note content with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate NoteSync:

  • Groq (whisper-large-v3): Processes audio for voice-to-text transcription. Audio is sent to Groq's API over HTTPS and is not retained after transcription. Groq's privacy policy applies to this processing.
  • Contabo VPS: Our servers are hosted on Contabo infrastructure in Germany. Your data is stored on servers we control and operate.

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or social login providers that would share your data with third parties.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2+ (HTTPS). Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a cost factor of 12. Your note data is stored in a PostgreSQL database on servers we control. See our Security page for full technical details.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Deleted notes are soft-deleted (marked as deleted) and purged from backups within 90 days.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Export your notes (contact support for a data export).
  • Object to processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@notesync.app. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

NoteSync is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification or email. Continued use of NoteSync after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
